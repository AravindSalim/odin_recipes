Tea

Descrition

Tea is one of the popular drinks in the world. I personally like strong chai which is a variation of TEA

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Tea powder
  • Sugar if needed

Steps:

  1. Mix Milk and Water, and boil
  2. Once boiled add tea and boil for few minutes
  3. Add sugar
  4. Servce in a glass hot
